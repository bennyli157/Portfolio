Programs used:

Photoshop

Overview Goal

the goal of this poster is to show my skills of combining images through masking to fit the objects and backgrounds together for photoshop. The poster shows my general the understanding of vectors, text, painting, masking, etc.

Process

For my poster, the instructor asked us to create a poster for critique. Selecting the theme took some time, but I eventually settled on using imagery from one of my beloved games to manipulate in Photoshop. Initially, I experimented with close-up shots of people in cosplay, but found it either overly intricate or not aligned with the intended tone. Instead, I opted for an image featuring an action figure, which better suited the vision I had in mind.

After selecting the image, I incorporated online textures into a vector corridor I designed. Using custom brushes, I added textures to the action figure for a weathered, metallic appearance. Finding suitable fonts for the title, I selected textures and introduced shadows and smoke to enhance the atmosphere, ensuring necessary masking along the way. By the end of the first week, most of the poster was complete. Following the instructor's feedback, I addressed lighting issues and included additional burnt metal textures. By week's end, minor adjustments were made to refine any inconsistencies in textures or lighting.

*Effects used for the logo

Results

Once I was done, my poster ended up as a nice homage to the one of the game's levels. All of which were used from photos and tools within the photoshop program.
