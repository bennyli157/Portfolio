-3D Graphic Design

3D Poster

Programs used:

Photoshop

Illustrator

Dimensions

Overview Goal

The Project is intended to demonstrate my proficiency of Adobe Dimensions by showcasing my abilities to import textures and illustrator files to create lifelike 3d objects.

Process

Initially, I discovered a suitable flat box model for the poster. Using this as a base, I crafted an image derived from my logo, intending it for placement on the box. Once the design was finalized, I folded the box into its intended shape and imported it into Adobe Dimensions.

After integrating a bottle into the design, I meticulously added textures to enhance realism, culminating in the completion phase where I perfected the placement angles for the final render.

*Behind the scenes of the 3d program

Results

The project culminated in a lifelike 3D image featuring a collection of meticulously designed objects alongside a photo background found online.
